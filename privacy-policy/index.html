Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

WearMe AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the WearMe AI mobile application (the "App"), you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address and profile photo to create and secure your account.

Photos and images you provide

To power virtual try-on, AI Fashion Studio and photo enhancement, you upload photos of yourself and images of clothing (uploaded, captured in-store, or imported from a shopping link). These images are processed to generate your results.

Usage & device data

We collect basic app usage, diagnostics and device information to keep the App reliable, secure and to improve our features.

Purchases

When you buy credits, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a purchase confirmation token to grant your credits — we never see or store your full payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate virtual try-on images, AI photoshoots and enhanced photos you request.
  • To create and manage your account, wardrobe and credit balance.
  • To process and deliver purchases made through Google Play.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse and provide customer support.
  • To improve and develop new features.

3. AI Processing

The images you upload are sent securely to trusted AI providers (including Google's Gemini models) to generate your results. Your photos are used only to produce the output you requested and to operate the App's features. We do not sell your images or personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account, wardrobe and generated looks are stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage) with industry-standard security. Access to your data is restricted to your own account, and credit balances are managed on secure servers you cannot tamper with. We use encryption in transit to protect your information.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g. Google Firebase, AI processing providers, Google Play) strictly to operate the App.
  • Legal authorities where required by law or to protect our rights and users.

6. Data Retention & Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App via Profile → Delete My Account. When you request deletion, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion and your data is permanently removed after a 30-day grace period. For full details, see our Delete Account page.

7. Children's Privacy

WearMe AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. You can manage most of this directly in the App, or contact us for assistance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at harshadsalunke2002@gmail.com.
